Frequently Asked

Crazy enough... yes! RAWR CON is in fact happening. Please note that this is a private event open only to team members of have literally been to hell and back together. We're also requiring that anyone who wishes to stay reserves a spot on the registry.
Jer has collected good faith deposits from enough participants to reserve a beautiful property with enough room for 16 guests comfortably. A deposit has already been made, but final payment in full is due in July. A mostly-final headcount will be used in July to calculate final pricing. This will be communicated out to everyone who has submitted a good faith deposit.
We'll be gathering in beautiful South Haven, Michigan.
Pack the usuals: clothing for a few days, toiletries, extra socks, a hoodie or sweater, maybe a rain coat or a wind breaker. The weather could be chilly and wet, with typical temps between 47-63F during our stay, we've seen warmer and colder weather. It's Michigan, you never know what you're going to
Please direct all requests to host or set up an event to Jer via Discord. Your info will be collected and updated to this page as soon as possible.
